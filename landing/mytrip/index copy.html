How to connect to
the service?

Receiving QR codes
Employees register themselves on the service website. Individual employees are issued a personal QR code and is issued for enrollment
Placement of QR codes
The service will independently develop layouts for printing table tents, stickers, posters, business cards with a QR code for employees, which are placed in prominent places in the institution or integrated into cash receipts
Payment via QR code
The client scans the QR code with a regular smartphone camera or an application for reading QR codes, goes to the personal page of the employee/team on the service website, where payment is made and feedback is sent about the quality of service, products, services
Payment options
Guests pay tips via Apple Pay / Google Pay payment systems, or by bank cards, through the secure payment gateway of the acquiring bank
